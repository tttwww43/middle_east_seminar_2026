Overview

This seminar is held during Medical Korea 2026 and provides a prestigious venue for academic exchange and networking for Middle East Medical Training Program participants.

Linked Event

Medical Korea 2026 (2026.3.19 – 3.22), the premier global healthcare exhibition in Seoul.

Venue

COEX Seoul – Conference Room (2F), Gangnam district, premium convention center.

Approximately 2 hours 20 minutes of intensive academic sessions and networking.

Three pillars of professional development: academic exchange, experience sharing, and strategic networking.

Faculty share training supervision experiences and present latest clinical techniques and medical trends.

II

Experience Sharing

Trainees share their medical training experiences in Korea, fostering cross-cultural medical understanding.

Curated networking session and exclusive lucky draw with trainees and key stakeholders.
